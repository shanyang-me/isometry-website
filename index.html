What Is Invariant Lab?

Invariant Lab builds AI that understands the invariant laws of physics—conservation of energy, momentum, symmetry under transformation. Our engine reasons about the physical world using a large language model, identifying objects, materials, and forces—then predicting what will happen next.

The result is a structured JSON output that downstream systems can use for physics-constrained image generation, robotics planning, or scene understanding.

Built on Invariant—named after the brain's invariant copy mechanism, its internal prediction of sensory consequences before an action occurs.

How It Works

Four stages from pixels to physics.

1

Image Input

Upload an image or describe a scene in natural language.

2

Visual Encoding

DINO + CLIP extract spatial and semantic features from the scene.

3

Physics Brain

An LLM identifies objects, computes forces, and predicts trajectories.

4

Guided Generation

Physics constraints guide diffusion to produce physically-plausible outputs.
